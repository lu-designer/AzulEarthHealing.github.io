My Story

Hi my name is Grant James, I started doing energy work and wholistic therapies in 1993 after being critically injured in a high speed motorcycle accident. I fractured my pelvis, broke my right leg, had many internal injuries and multiple surgeries over the next year. I needed to learn how to walk again at the age of 26.

3 days after the accident 2 doctors were telling me how lucky I was to be alive. I responded with, “I don’t feel that lucky at the moment”. They then let me know that they don’t know how I survived my injuries, they both said if it was them in my place, they would have died.

Suddenly I realised we are more than just physical beings, I was open to the fact that I am a lot more than I supposed myself to be. If I can survive this, what else is possible?

This is when my path to healing and spiritual awakening started. I spent the next years learning energy work, massage and body work, focusing on nutrition and a clean diet, learning emotional clearing, NLP and understanding how the human mind operates. Practising Yoga, Qi Gong, using essential oils, spending time in nature as part of my rehabilitation - like walking up hills, swimming in the ocean, camping and spending time in nature and spending time in sacred places.

This led me to learning about Shamanic principles, reading Carlos Castaneda books, doing sweat lodges and questioning reality as I once perceived it. I still follow the Shamanic path and it seems normal for people on this path to have a 20 to 30 year process back to wellness from a major event, and in that process discover and learn many things to heal themselves and in turn help other people.

Now that I have been working on human bodies for about 30 years, I have turned my attention to helping them shift unproductive energies in the spaces where they live or reside.

Sometimes people's ailments are because of the place or area where they live. EMF (Electrical Magnetic Radiation) and electrical radiation are a chaotic frequency to the human body. Geopathic stresses like stagnant underground water, sleeping on top of where a significant tree used to be, residing where a lot of earth works have taken place or living over an earthquake line, can also cause an imbalance to the human body, as do negative Ley Lines.

The way I like to frame it is: “How do you feel after being in nature, camping near a river over a weekend, after visiting a waterfall, swimming in the ocean, visiting an amazing sacred site, hugging a tree or visiting your favourite forest?”

The human body is naturally attracted to the frequency and vibration of nature. When a house or land is out of balance it can be restored and maintained using crystals. Earth Healing restores this balance.
