My Story

Hi, my name is Grant James.

I began doing energy work and holistic therapies in 1993 after being critically injured in a high-speed motorcycle accident. I fractured my pelvis, broke my right leg, sustained many internal injuries, and had multiple surgeries over the following year. At the age of 26, I had to learn how to walk again.

Three days after the accident, two doctors told me how lucky I was to be alive. I responded, “I don’t feel that lucky at the moment.” They then told me they did not know how I had survived my injuries. Both said that if they had been in my place, they believed they would have died.

Something shifted in me then. I suddenly realised that we are more than just physical beings. I became open to the fact that I was far more than I had previously supposed myself to be. If I could survive this, what else was possible?
